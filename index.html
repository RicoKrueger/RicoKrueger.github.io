About me

My name is Rico Krueger and I am currently a Ph.D. candidate in Civil & Environmental Engineering at UNSW Australia. My doctoral research is supervised by TH Rashidi, ST Waller and A Vij , and focusses on the development and application of new flexible mixture models for discrete choice analysis. The aim of my research is to fuse the conceptual benefits of mixed random utility models with flexible statistical methods for the representation of unobserved heterogeneity.

Prior to joining UNSW Australia, I completed both a Master of Science and Bachelor of Science in Industrial Engineering & Management at TU Berlin. My studies at TU Berlin were accompanied by a student exchange to UNSW Australia as well as by professional experiences in industry, academia and inter-governmental cooperation in the domains of transport and mobility.
